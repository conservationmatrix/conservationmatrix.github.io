Investing Made Easy

Conservation Matrix makes funding real asset projects easy. Whether it be timber, sustainable agriculture, or green infrastructure, we provide a platform for investors, project developers and foundations to work together.

How projects get funded through Conservation Matrix

Project developers submit their deal proposals

Ideas are submitted along with basic finacials to the Conservation Matrix team.

Conservation Matrix’s algorithm and experts advance the most financially and environmentally promising deals

Conservation Matrix's team of finance and enviornmental experts remove about 80% of projects leaving only 20% of projects to be seen by investors and donors.

Projects are posted to Conservation Matrix for public viewing

Projects are displayed with relevant financial and environmental information for funders to gain an understanding of project economics and impact.

Interested funders request more detail

Interested and qualified funders can then request detail on deals they like to gain access to a confidential data room. Here they can also converse with other donors and structure a deal with the help of Conservation Matrix.

Deal closes and funds are transferred

Funders review and sign documentation and transfer funds – all through the Conservation Matrix website.
